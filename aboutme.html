WHO AM I?

WHY I CHOSE WEB DEVELOPMENT?

My journey into the IT field started with a curiosity about how the internet actually works. I love the instant gratification of seeing code turn into a visual interface and the endless puzzles that come with debugging. For me, web development is the perfect intersection of logic and creativity, and I’m constantly looking for new technologies to learn and integrate into my work.

WHAT DO I DO BESIDE CODING?

When I’m not deep in a code editor or studying database schemas, you can usually find me exploring the world away from the screen. I’m a big believer in staying active and finding balance—whether that’s through fitness, discovering new spots around Winnipeg, or spending quality time with my family. Taking breaks from the digital world helps me return to my projects with a fresh perspective and a clear mind.
